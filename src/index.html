WHO WE ARE

Residential Construction

We have been specialising in residential buildings since 2012. We have built nearly 300 homes, including luxurious family homes, affordable residential houses and flats, care and retirement accommodations and housing for sale and private rent.

We are proud of the positive impact that our homes have on the community. While we have an extensive range of residential work and a vast client base, we focus on producing a fantastic home for our clients to enjoy.

To ensure that we provide the best service to our customers, we work in a ‘one company’ approach meaning that our customers can benefit from full access to a range of skills, expertise, and know-how to deliver the best housing.

Our managing director Alex Strikovs and Max Falko, our construction manager, work seamlessly to offer our customers the best residential houses.
